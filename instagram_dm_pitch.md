# Instagram DM Sales Pitch — Curtains & Blinds Dr

> **Context:** You (Seasons® Design Studios) are reaching out to Curtains & Blinds Dr via Instagram DM. You've already built them a fully functional luxury e-commerce website and comprehensive brand identity system. You'll attach a cinematic walkthrough video of the live website to the very first message. The goal is to close them as a long-term paying client.

---

## 🎬 Pre-DM Warm-Up (Do This First)

Before sending the DM:
1. **Follow** their Instagram account
2. **Engage genuinely** with 2–3 of their recent posts — leave thoughtful comments about their work, not generic emojis
3. **Wait 24–48 hours** so your name is familiar when the DM lands
4. **Record your video** — a smooth 30–60 second walkthrough of the website: hero slideshow → collections → product page. No voiceover needed, let the design speak. Add subtle background music if possible.

---

## 🔥 The DM Sequence (3-Part, Video-Led)

### Message 1 — The Video Hook (Send First)

**Attach the website walkthrough video**, then type:

> Hey team! 👋 I came across Curtains & Blinds Dr and the quality of your work genuinely stood out to me — the bespoke approach, in-home consultations, the premium fabrics. You're clearly not just another curtains company.
>
> I couldn't help myself — I went ahead and built this for you. No catch, no strings. Just hit play. ▶️
>
> This is a fully functional luxury website designed specifically around your brand — your products, your process, your story.
>
> Would love to hear what you think 🙏

> [!TIP]
> **Why video-first works:** A 30-second video is worth 10,000 words in a DM. It bypasses the "who is this person trying to sell me something" reflex and goes straight to "wait… is that OUR brand?" — pure emotional impact before any sales conversation.

---

### Message 2 — The Breakdown (Send After They Respond)

> Glad it caught your eye! Let me break down what's in there:
>
> **🌐 The Website:**
> - Cinematic hero slideshow showcasing your Sheer, Blackout, Roman, Roller & Motorised ranges
> - Full product catalogue with fabric swatches, size selectors, and instant quote flow
> - "Book Free Consultation" conversion funnel on every page
> - Instagram grid integration pulling your latest posts
> - Client testimonials, your 4-step process, and editorial storytelling sections
> - Mobile-responsive, SEO-ready, lightning fast — zero frameworks slowing it down
>
> **🎨 The Brand Identity:**
> - Refined warm neutral palette with your signature accent colour
> - Playfair Display + Inter typography system — sophistication meets readability
> - Glassmorphism UI elements that feel modern and premium
> - Cohesive visual language from logo lockup to footer
>
> Everything you saw in that video is real and ready to deploy. Not a mockup. Not a template. Built from scratch for Curtains & Blinds Dr.

---

### Message 3 — The Close (Send After They Engage)

> Here's the thing — a beautiful website is just the starting point. Let me paint the full picture:
>
> 📈 **More booked consultations** — Right now potential clients see your Instagram, get interested, but have nowhere premium to land. This site turns that interest into actual bookings.
>
> 💰 **Higher perceived value** — When a client is choosing between you and a competitor, they'll pick the business that *looks* like the premium option. This positions you as THE luxury choice.
>
> 🔄 **Continuous growth** — Not a once-off. A partnership.
>
> **Phase 1 — Deploy Now:**
> - Launch the website
> - Integrate booking & Instagram feed
> - SEO setup for "custom curtains Johannesburg", "blinds Cape Town", etc.
>
> **Phase 2 — Monthly Retainer:**
> - Website maintenance & new collection pages
> - Seasonal campaign landing pages
> - Performance analytics & conversion optimisation
> - Social media alignment
>
> I invested the time upfront because the potential here is clear. The only question is whether you're ready to stop leaving money on the table.
>
> When are you free for a quick 15-minute call this week? I'll walk you through everything properly. 📞

---

## 🧠 Why This Sequence Wins

| Element | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| **Video in DM 1** | Creates an instant "wow" — they see their own brand elevated before reading a single word of sales copy |
| **No ask in DM 1** | Zero pressure. "Would love to hear what you think" is disarming and invites genuine conversation |
| **Breakdown in DM 2** | Transitions from emotional to logical — now they understand the depth of what was built |
| **Phased proposal in DM 3** | Phase 1 is tangible and immediate; Phase 2 positions ongoing value (recurring revenue for you) |
| **"Not a mockup" line** | Eliminates the #1 objection before they can think it |
| **Their own language** | "Bespoke", "in-home consultations", "premium fabrics" — mirrors their brand voice back to them |

---

## 💡 Delivery Tips

1. **Best time to send DM 1:** Tuesday–Wednesday, 9am–11am SAST
2. **Video format:** MP4, under 60 seconds, 1080p or 4K. No voiceover — let the design speak. Light ambient music optional
3. **Patience:** Wait for their reply before sending DM 2. Never stack all 3 messages at once
4. **48hr no-reply fallback:** Send a 20-second voice note: *"Hey! Just wanted to make sure you saw what I sent — I'd genuinely love your feedback on it"*
5. **If they ask about pricing:** *"I'd rather understand your full needs first so I can put together something that makes sense for your business. That's what the 15-minute call is for."* — Always move pricing to a call

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **The video is your secret weapon.** Every other agency sends paragraphs of text. You're sending proof. That's the difference between getting left on read and getting a reply within the hour.
