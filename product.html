Our Ivory Sheer Linen is woven from 100% European flax linen using traditional methods. The natural, unbleached fibers create a soft, organic glow when backlit by sunlight. Each panel features a delicate drape with a gentle crinkle texture that adds depth and character to any space.

Composition: 100% European Linen
Weight: 120 gsm
Width: 150 cm

This sheer fabric allows approximately 70% of natural light to pass through, creating a warm, diffused glow in your room. It provides daytime privacy while maintaining an open, airy feel. For enhanced privacy or light control, consider adding a privacy or blackout lining.

Dry clean recommended for best results. If machine washing, use a gentle cycle at 30°C with a mesh laundry bag. Hang to dry — do not tumble dry. Iron on low heat while slightly damp for best results. Steam cleaning is also suitable.

Professional installation is available for R799 per window. Our skilled installers will fit your curtains with precision, ensuring a perfect hang. Installation includes all necessary hardware. Alternatively, each curtain comes with a standard 7.5 cm heading tape for easy self-installation.

Custom-made curtains are delivered within 10–14 working days. Standard delivery is free for orders over R2,500. Express production is available for an additional R499, reducing the lead time to 5–7 working days. Fabric samples can be ordered free of charge.
